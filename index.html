HI-rich Failed Halos — Analog Galaxy Data

Simulated analogs to Cloud-9 from FIREbox, NIVARIA-LG, and Recal-EAGLE

This table contains galaxy-level properties for analog halos selected from three cosmological simulations. Each row is one galaxy; columns give the base-10 logarithm of halo mass (M200), total gas mass (Mgas), and neutral hydrogen mass (MHI), all in solar masses.

These data underlie the corner-plot figure comparing the three simulations in the M200–Mgas–MHI parameter space.

Gas Phase Diagrams

Temperature–density phase diagram data for all four FIREbox halos pooled together, compared to NIVARIA-LG and Recal-EAGLE. The IQR bands show the 25th–75th percentile temperature in 0.3 dex hydrogen number density bins. The ratio panel (bottom) shows log₁₀(T / TBL+2017), where TBL+2017 is the Benitez-Llambay & Frenk (2017) photoionisation equilibrium reference curve.
